## 04 — Video Ads (5 briefs for Meta)

### Shared specs

- **Formats**: 9:16 primary; also export 4:5 and 1:1
- **Length**: 15s primary (make 20–30s version only for Ad 3)
- **Style**: UGC selfie + b-roll overlays (results UI mock, kit unboxing, simple steps)
- **Captions**: burned-in + `.srt`
- **CTA**: “Tap to see what’s included” / “Tap to check availability”

### Required disclosure line (safe default)

- “For informational purposes only. Not medical advice.”

---

## Video Ad 1 — Clarity / “Know your numbers”

### Structure (beats)
1) **Hook (0–2s)**: selfie, close-up
   - Overlay: “At-home blood test (simple snapshot)”
   - VO: “If you’ve been feeling ‘off’, this is the easiest first step.”
2) **Problem (2–6s)**: quick cut to calendar / waiting room meme
   - VO: “Most people don’t want a whole appointment just to get basic clarity.”
3) **Proof (6–12s)**: kit on table + 3-step graphic
   - Overlay: “Order → follow steps → view results”
4) **CTA (12–15s)**: thumb taps “See what’s included”
   - VO: “Tap to see what’s included and whether it fits your goals.”

### Shot list
- Shot 1: selfie hook (bright light, simple background)
- Shot 2: quick b-roll “waiting room / calendar”
- Shot 3: kit + 3-step overlay + CTA tap

## Video Ad 3 — Education / “Results explained” (20–30s)

### Structure (beats)
1) **Hook (0–2s)**: split-screen: confusing lab report vs clean UI
   - Overlay: “Understand your results”
   - VO: “The best part isn’t the test—it’s understanding the results.”
2) **Problem (2–7s)**: highlight messy numbers / jargon
   - VO: “A lot of lab reports are confusing. That’s why people ignore them.”
3) **Proof (7–22s)**: show “simple categories” UI mock + “what’s included” list
   - Overlay: “Clearer view of key markers”
   - VO: “This starts with an at-home kit, then presents your results in a simpler way so you can take informed next steps.”
4) **CTA (22–30s)**: CTA tap
   - VO: “Tap to see the results format and what’s included.”

### Shot list
- Shot 1: lab report vs clean UI mock
- Shot 2: highlight confusing sections
- Shot 3: simple UI mock + list + CTA
