## 02 — Ad Scripts (5 angles)

### Global rules (policy-safe language)

- Do not diagnose (“you have…”), do not guarantee outcomes.
- Use: “may help”, “for informational purposes”, “not medical advice”.
- Emphasize process + clarity + convenience.

---

## Ad 1 — “Know your numbers” (Clarity / peace of mind)

### Master script (15s)
- **Hook (0–2s)**:
  - Line: “If you’ve been feeling ‘off’, this is the easiest first step.”
  - Overlay: “At-home blood test (simple snapshot)”
- **Setup (2–6s)**:
  - “Most people don’t want a whole appointment just to get basic clarity.”
- **Proof (6–12s)**:
  - “This kit lets you check key markers from home, then see your results in one place.”
- **CTA (12–15s)**:
  - “Tap to see what’s included and whether it fits your goals.”
- **Disclosures**:
  - “Not medical advice. Results vary.”

### Hook variants (3)
1) **Authority**: “A lot of health ‘mysteries’ start with missing basic lab info.”
2) **Contrarian**: “You don’t need a full doctor visit to start getting clarity.”
3) **Proof**: “See your numbers in a simple dashboard—no guessing.”

### Copy blocks
- **Primary text**:
  - “Get a clear snapshot of key health markers from home. Not medical advice.”
  - “Curious about your numbers? Start with a simple at-home kit.”
- **Headline**:
  - “See What’s In The Kit”
  - “Know Your Numbers”
  - “At-Home Test (Simple)”
