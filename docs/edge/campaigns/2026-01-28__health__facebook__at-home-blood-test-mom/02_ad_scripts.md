## 02 — Ad scripts (RSOC-safe direct response)

**Persona reference (internal)**: “running on empty / poor sleep / brain fog”  
**Copy persona**: *The Calm Contrarian Operator* (see `docs/edge/workflows/ad-copy-framework.md`)

### Policy hard gates (must pass)

- No diagnosis / certainty
- No “you have…” / “your condition…” / “are you suffering…”
- No guarantees (“will”, “instant”, “cure”)
- Claims must be supported by the article
- CTA is micro-commitment (“see the checklist / see the 3 steps”)
- Disclosure handling: keep disclaimers out of headlines; use “For informational purposes” as tiny footer text only if required.
