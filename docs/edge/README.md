## What “Edge” is

`Edge` is our **buyer ops workspace**: ad accounts, campaign launch setups, naming conventions, checklists, and experiment logs.

## Where to put things

- `meta-accounts.md`: Business portfolios, ad accounts, pixel/dataset, permissions, and “where the campaign lives”.
- `checklists/initial-launch.md`: Step-by-step launch protocol checklist (practical, not theory).
- `workflows/campaign-workflow.md`: The repeatable “Category → Script → Keywords → Video → Setup” process.
- `personas/edge-portfolio-growth-buyer-roas.md`: The ROAS-optimized buyer persona + decision rules (learning mode, scaling, cut rules).
- `templates/campaign-packet/`: Templates used to create a new campaign packet folder.
- `campaigns/`: Per-campaign notes (setup, hypotheses, key decisions, and links).

## Daily operating rule

If we touch a campaign/account, we log **one** of:
- the account id/name
- the campaign id/name
- the exact navigation path/link

…so future you can pick up instantly.
