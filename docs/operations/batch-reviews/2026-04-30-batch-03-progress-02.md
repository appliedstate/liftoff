# Batch 03 Progress Review — Meaningful Change 02

This packet is designed for operator review in under two minutes.

## Change 02

### Change

Built the opportunity supply quality loop so upstream exploration is now scored by owner, source, and category using launch conversion, blueprint coverage, stale pending pressure, and closed-loop rates.

### Responsible Board Seat

`Elon Musk`

Why this seat:

This change optimizes for compounding territory acquisition rather than more passive reporting. It asks whether the supply engine is generating usable fuel, not merely activity.

### Why It Needs To Exist

Before this change, the system could show:

- how many opportunities existed
- who owned some of them
- how much predicted upside was still pending

But it still could not answer whether the exploration engine was producing quality supply.

That meant the system could see inventory without knowing if the inventory was actually becoming blueprint-backed and launch-proven work.

### Why Now

After building buyer daily command packets, the next leverage point was improving the quality of what those packets eventually direct buyers toward.

If the upstream supply loop is low quality, even good buyer command packets just steer people into stale or weak inventory.

### Limiting Factor It Tackles

Primary limiting factor:

`the system could measure opportunity volume, but not opportunity quality`

More specifically:

it could not distinguish between healthy supply creation and a queue that was just filling with predicted upside that never closes.

### Operator Read

This change should let the operator answer:

1. Is our opportunity supply actually converting?
2. Which owners are producing quality upstream flow versus stale inventory?
3. Which sources and categories are healthy versus noisy?
4. Is the current bottleneck more opportunity quantity or opportunity quality?
